Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Eggs 4
Pancetta or Guanciale 200g
Parmesan Cheese 100g

Step 1: Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.

Step 2: Cut pancetta into small pieces and fry until crispy.

Step 3: Mix eggs with grated Parmesan cheese.

Step 4: Drain pasta and toss with hot pancetta and its fat.

Step 5: Remove from heat and add egg mixture, stirring quickly.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 24 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 225g
Brown Sugar 200g
Eggs 2
Flour 280g
Chocolate Chips 200g

Step 2: Cream butter and brown sugar together.

Step 3: Beat in eggs one at a time.

Step 4: Mix in flour and chocolate chips.

Step 5: Drop spoonfuls onto baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
